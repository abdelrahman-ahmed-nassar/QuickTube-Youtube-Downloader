Frequently Asked Questions

Is QuickTube free?

Yes! QuickTube is 100% free and open source under the MIT license. No hidden costs, no subscriptions.

Does it require installation?

Nope! QuickTube is a single executable file. Just download and run. No installation, no Python, no dependencies needed.

Is it safe to use?

Absolutely. QuickTube is open source, so you can review the code yourself. It uses trusted tools like yt-dlp and ffmpeg under the hood.

Where are downloaded files saved?

All files are saved to an "output" folder in the same directory as the QuickTube executable. Clean and organized.

Does it support playlists?

Yes! You can download entire YouTube playlists or choose to download individual videos from a playlist.

What video qualities are available?

You can download videos in 360p (low), 720p (medium), or 1080p (high) quality. For audio, choose from 50kbps, 128kbps, or 192kbps.
